---
title: Durango Cowboy Gathering
event_date: Late September / Early October
location: Downtown Durango
summary: Cowboy poetry, music, and a Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Railroad "Cowboy Poetry Train."
---
The Durango Cowboy Gathering brings cowboy poetry, western music, and a dedicated "Cowboy Poetry Train" run by the Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Railroad to town each late September or early October. It's a strong shoulder-season draw for the Durango area.
