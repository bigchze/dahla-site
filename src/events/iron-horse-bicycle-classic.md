---
The Iron Horse Bicycle Classic takes place over Memorial Day weekend each year, with cyclists racing the Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Railroad train along the route from Durango to Silverton. It's one of the region's signature annual events and a major driver of lodging demand — member properties should plan staffing and inventory accordingly well in advance.
