| Organization | What they cover |
|---|---|
| [Visit Durango](https://www.durango.org) | Official destination marketing organization — visitor guides, event grants, and the "Care for Durango" responsible-tourism pledge. |
| [Durango Chamber of Commerce](https://www.durangobusiness.org) | General business advocacy and networking across all Durango industries. |
| [Durango Business Improvement District](https://www.downtowndurango.org) | Downtown & North Main property district — seasonal marketing campaigns and community events. |
| [City of Durango](https://www.durangoco.gov) | Lodgers'-tax administration, short-term rental permitting, and municipal government. |
| [La Plata County](https://www.co.laplata.co.us) | County lodging tax, the Lodging Tax Committee, and unincorporated-area regulation. |
| [Colorado Hotel & Lodging Association](https://www.chla.com) | Statewide lodging trade association and policy advocacy. |
