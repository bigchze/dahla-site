## What Membership Offers

- **Advocacy** — a united voice on lodgers'-tax allocation, short-term rental policy, and legislation affecting your business.
- **Education** — training on frontline guest service, compliance, leadership development, and HR/hiring topics.
- **Networking** — regular meetings with fellow Durango-area lodging and hospitality operators.
- **Information** — a shared resource network and clear communication — something our members told us directly they want more of.

Membership dues and eligibility details are being finalized. Reach out and we'll follow up directly: **[info@durangolodging.org](mailto:info@durangolodging.org)**

## Who Should Join

Hotels, motels, inns, bed & breakfasts, vacation rentals, campgrounds, and hospitality-adjacent businesses across the Durango area.

Not sure if you qualify? [Ask us](mailto:info@durangolodging.org) — we'll point you in the right direction.
